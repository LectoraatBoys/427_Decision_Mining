If a student has Dutch nationality or meets the legal conditions for residence in the Netherlands, he/she is eligible for student finance.
If a student is between 18 and 30 years old, he/she is entitled to student finance.
If a student follows a full-time MBO, HBO or WO course at a recognized educational institution, he/she is eligible for student finance.
If a student meets the requirements for study progress, such as obtaining a minimum number of credits per year, he/she will retain the right to student finance.
If the income of the student or his/her parents does not exceed the set income limits, he/she is eligible for a student grant.
